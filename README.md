# Acid Marble Experiment Micro-Asset

This micro-asset is part of the NEURAVERSE educational collection.  
Goal: To teach multi-disciplinary concepts of acids, chemical reactions, mass transfer, and the effect of acids on living tissues.

## Scientific Topics
- Chemistry: Acid–Carbonate reaction
- Classical Physics: Gas pressure, temperature, mass, volume
- Quantum Physics: Ionic bond breaking, electron behavior
- Mathematics: Measurement, ratio, reaction rate calculation
- Biology: Effect of acids on plant cells
- Materials Science: Marble resistance and crystal structure

## Target Age
10–15 years old
